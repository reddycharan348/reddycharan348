Real-World Example: Automating Lead Qualification & Outreach

The Challenge: Bottlenecks in Lead Handling

Manually qualifying leads from forms leads to delays, missed opportunities, and mountains of repetitive work. A streamlined, automated process is crucial for growth.

The n8n Workflow:

  • Trigger: New form submission (Typeform, Google Forms).
  • AI Analysis: Qualify leads using OpenAI via HTTP Request.
  • Logic: Conditional branching based on qualification score.
  • CRM Update: Sync qualified leads to Airtable or Google Sheets.
  • Personalized Email: Send tailored outreach via Gmail/SendGrid.
  • Notification: Alert your team on Slack/Teams.

This automated sequence ensures no lead is missed, qualifies them intelligently, updates your records, initiates communication, and keeps your team informed – all in real-time!
